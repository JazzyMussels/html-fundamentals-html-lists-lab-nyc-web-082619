• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Steps to Make that Good Good

  1. Spread butter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
    3. Add cheese on top of bread
  3. Cover with second slice of bread
    4. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
